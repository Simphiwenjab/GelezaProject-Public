About Us

What do we do?

Geleza Mzansi Online Applications operates by Broadening South African youth's tertiary pathways and therefore by playing a role of applying for students in highly experienced higher education and training institutions within the borders of South Africa.

We do that from the heart because we know that an educated and knowledgeable nation, is indeed a leading nation and We encourage the doing of everything now because sometimes 'later' becomes 'never'.

What change do we bring?

We are the proud and first South African applications office to assist and support the youth of the motherland academmically, through different Social network plattforms.

Where we supply academic materials includig past examination papers with their Memo's, study guides etc for all subjects covered by the South African Basic education Curriculum.

We do that because we know exactly that a pathway to tertiary does not end in being motivated and encouraged to apply but furthers by requiring reasonable grades to be admitted in desired carrer path.
